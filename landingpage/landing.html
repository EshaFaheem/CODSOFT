    Frequently asked Questions

    Do you offer delivery services for flowers?

    Yes, we provide flower delivery services. You can have flowers delivered to your desired location for special occasions or as gifts.

    Do you provide same-day flower delivery?

    We do offer same-day delivery for orders placed within a specific time frame. Please check with our shop for the cutoff time in your area.

    What types of occasions do you provide flowers for?

    We cater to a wide range of occasions, including birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, funerals, holidays, and more

    Can I request custom flower arrangements?

    Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom flower arrangements tailored to your preferences and specific occasions. Just let us know your requirements.

    Do you offer seasonal flower options?

    Yes, we have seasonal flower selections throughout the year, so you can choose flowers that are in bloom and perfectly suited for the current season.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Please check with our shop for specific payment options.

    Can I order flowers for international delivery?

    While we primarily focus on local and national deliveries, we can sometimes arrange for international flower deliveries. Please inquire about specific international delivery options.

    What is your return or refund policy?

    We have a return and refund policy in place. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within a reasonable timeframe, and we will assist you with the process.

    Do you offer gift wrapping services for flower arrangements?

    Yes, we can provide gift wrapping or special packaging for your flower arrangements to make your gift even more beautiful.

    Are your flowers sourced sustainably and locally when possible?

    We prioritize sustainability and local sourcing whenever feasible, and we make an effort to provide eco-friendly options for our customers.
